# Brag Plan: Mira — "Close deals in your sleep" (vertical, fast)

## What is this app?
Mira is the AI that runs your Instagram. This cut tells ONE story: the money hiding in your DMs. Mira reads every message, detects the brand deals and collabs, and closes them — automatically.

## The angle
A fast, vertical, keynote-grade flex about closing deals. Pure black, floating glass, but cut quick — snap reveals, tight holds, staccato "WON" hits, and a single big-number payoff. No storefront, no general "replies" — every beat is about turning DMs into closed money.

## Hook (first 2 seconds)
A real, lucrative DM snaps onto black: **"got a collab budget — $5k? 👀"** with a soft indigo bloom. The viewer's brain finishes the sentence: *that's a deal you'd miss.* Fast.

## Key moments (the middle)
- **Mira detects it** — the DM resolves into a deal card: Brand Deal · 92% confident · $4,000.
- **It closes — fast** — three stacked deal cards slam to **WON** in quick succession ($4,000 / $2,800 / $6,500), green glow per hit.
- **The payoff** — one giant number counts up to **$23,400 closed this month**.

## Outro / punchline
Sunburst + **Mira** + **Close deals in your sleep.**

## User flow worth showing
The deal-closing flow only: DM arrives → Mira detects a deal → deal card hits WON → total closed. Real Mira Opportunities surfaces (confidence %, value, Won state), staged fast.

## Tone
- Preset: cinematic (fast variant)
- Creative direction (verbatim): "Apple keynote meets Linear and Arc Browser. Cinematic black backgrounds, floating UI elements, elegant typography, slow dramatic reveals, subtle ambient sound, premium motion design and world-class SaaS craftsmanship. just talk about closing deals and make video fasttttt and in vertical reel ratio"
- Interpretation: keep the black/glass/glow keynote look, but PACE IS FAST — snappy reveals (0.4–0.7s), tight holds (~1s), staccato WON hits on the beat, a quick count-up. Energetic, not sleepy. Reads in a vertical feed.

## Format: vertical — 1080x1920
## Duration: ~16s (fast)

## Visual identity (from the project)
- Background: pure black `#000000` + faint dot-grid + center bloom + vignette
- Floating glass: `rgba(255,255,255,0.05)` fill, `rgba(255,255,255,0.10)` border, soft shadow + indigo bloom; WON state = green `#3f9e63` glow
- Accent: `#4f6bed` Mira indigo; close/win = green `#3f9e63`
- Text: `#f5f5f5` / muted; tracked uppercase accent kickers; huge tabular number for payoff
- Display font: Inter 800 tight; 500 wide-tracked uppercase labels
- Strongest visual: glass deal cards hitting WON; the big count-up number; the sunburst.

## Share copy (draft)
There's a brand deal sitting in your DMs right now. Mira finds it, qualifies it, and closes it — while you sleep. 🖤💸

## Storyboard

### Scene 1 — Hook: the DM — 2.4s
Black. A glass DM bubble snaps in (fast scale + bloom): **"got a collab budget — $5k? 👀"**. Tracked kicker under it: **THERE'S MONEY IN YOUR DMs**. Tiny Mira mark top.
Sequential/interaction: bubble snaps, kicker follows fast.
Audio intent: ambient in + one soft tick on the snap.
Transition: quick dip → Scene 2

### Scene 2 — Detect — 3.0s
The bubble lifts to the top; below it a deal card materializes with a connecting glow: **Brand Deal · 92% confident · $4,000**. Kicker: **MIRA DETECTS THE DEAL**.
Sequential/interaction: card resolves from the message fast.
Audio intent: soft resolve tick.
Transition: quick dip → Scene 3

### Scene 3 — Close, fast — 4.2s
Three glass deal cards stacked vertically. Each slams to **WON** in quick succession with a green glow flash and value: **$4,000 / $2,800 / $6,500**. Kicker: **CLOSED.**
Sequential/interaction: yes — three WON hits on the beat grid (8.02 / 8.74 / 9.29), staccato.
Audio intent: a clean warm chime per WON; tight, punchy.
Transition: hard-ish cut → Scene 4

### Scene 4 — The payoff number — 3.0s
Everything clears to black. One giant tabular number counts up fast to **$23,400** with label **CLOSED THIS MONTH**. Big indigo→green bloom on the land.
Sequential/interaction: yes — count-up 0 → $23,400, finishing on the strong cue (~10.93), one big hit.
Audio intent: rising, then one big warm chime on the final number.
Transition: quick dip → Scene 5

### Scene 5 — Outro — 2.6s
Sunburst blooms + **Mira** + line **Close deals in your sleep.**
Sequential/interaction: fast bloom + line.
Audio intent: resolve; low chime, music fades.
Transition: fade → end (black)

**Music mood:** cinematic-ambient, a touch louder + punchier for a fast vertical cut.
**Audio summary:** Ambient bed in fast, three clean WON chimes in quick succession, one big hit on the $23,400 payoff, then a soft resolve chime under the outro as music fades.
