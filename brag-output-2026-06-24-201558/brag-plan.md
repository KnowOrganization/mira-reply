# Brag Plan: Mira — Launch Film

## What is this app?
Mira is a local AI that runs a creator's Instagram inbox — it reads incoming DMs and comments, understands intent, replies in the creator's own voice (English or Hinglish), spots brand deals worth money, and turns conversations into sales. "Local AI for Instagram, in your voice."

## The angle
A premium SaaS launch film that walks the **entire user journey** as a keynote reveal: the flood of DMs → Mira reading intent → a reply written in your voice → a brand deal detected → the outcomes (engagement up, hours saved, zero flags). Educate at every beat while looking like a Stripe Sessions / Vercel Ship / Linear keynote cut. Dark glass on pure black, indigo Mira accent, fast confident motion, beat-locked reveals.

## Hook (first 2-3 seconds)
Black. A single indigo radial burst blooms (the real Mira logo mark), the word **Mira** rises under it, then the line *Local AI for Instagram, in your voice.* One clean confident hit on the downbeat. No clutter.

## Key moments (the middle)
- **The flood** — incoming DM bubbles cascade and stack, an "unread" badge ticks up. The problem, felt instantly.
- **Mira reads intent** — one DM isolates and floats up; a data-flow line runs into the Mira mark; intent chips light up one by one (Question · Buyer · Brand deal). The "AI understanding context" beat, made literal.
- **Replies in your voice** — the answer types out word-by-word in Hinglish, sends on the beat, with the callout *Unique every time · Never flagged.*
- **DMs into deals** — a deal card illuminates green and stamps **WON** with a ₹ value. Mira spots money in the inbox.
- **The outcome** — three stat cards count up one per beat: 94% replied · 3.2 hrs saved/day · 0 flags. Customer outcomes, keynote-style.

## Outro / punchline
Burst re-blooms, **Mira** returns, line: *Connect Instagram. Mira does the rest.*

## User flow worth showing
entry → key action → result:
1. DMs flood in (entry / problem).
2. Mira reads intent and replies in your voice (key action).
3. A deal is won and the outcome stats land (result).
This is the centerpiece — recreated as live floating UI (chat bubbles, intent chips, deal card, stat cards), not landing-page sections.

## Tone
- Preset: app-store (with cinematic restraint)
- Creative direction: premium SaaS launch film — Stripe Sessions × Vercel Ship × Linear × Cursor × Apple keynote; fast-paced motion graphics, floating interface elements, feature callouts, data flows, statistics, polished transitions.
- Interpretation: confident high pace driven by motion and tight beat-locked cuts, not by flashing text; every readable line slams in fast then holds to its floor; pure-black keynote stage with glass surfaces and one indigo accent keeps it premium, not busy.
